A LITTLE STORY ABOUT WHAT I DO IN MY LIFE

Hello, world! My name is CHUL KWON

ENTREPRENEUR | PRODUCT MANAGER | EARLY-STAGE INVESTOR

As an inquisitive polymath, I have studied multiple subjects -- notably Computer Science and Economics -- and led multidisciplinary teams composed of businesspeople, engineers, and scientists in management consulting projects and later my own company.

Short career story: I started programming in C at the age of 10. At 12 - 13, I studied industrial design, as well as design tools like Photoshop and Illustrator, for which I'm officially certified. Meanwhile, I picked up my knowledge in Economics mostly through reading books and newspapers. I finished my Economics degree (specialty: international trade and finance) within a year in university. I started my own company with two partners when I was a junior and afterwards, worked at Citi's fixed income division for a year. Currently, I work as the Head of Product at InterviewJet, but I'm also active in the Venture Capital community as a partner for The Council.
